About Us

Welcome to the Polytechnique Montreal Optics Student Chapter. We are a student-led organization dedicated to promoting optics and photonics through various activities, including student conferences, workshops, and networking events. Our chapter is part of the global Optica and SPIE network, providing students with opportunities to present their research, connect with professionals, and learn from experts in the field.
